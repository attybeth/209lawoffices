Welcome to 209 Law Offices

Located in the City of Cebu, 209 Law Offices is a shared professional address for five independent lawyers, each engaged in the private practice of law.

While we maintain individual legal practices, we work from a common office guided by shared values of discipline, discretion, and respect for the legal profession. This website provides general information about our office and serves as a point of contact for each practitioner.
