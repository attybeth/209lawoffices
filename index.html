Welcome to 209 Law Offices

Maligayang Pagdating sa 209 Law Offices

Located in the City of Cebu, 209 Law Offices is a shared professional address for five independent lawyers, each engaged in the private practice of law.

While we maintain individual legal practices, we work from a common office guided by shared values of discipline, discretion, and respect for the legal profession. This website provides general information about our office and serves as a point of contact for each practitioner.

Kindly note that client engagements are handled solely by the individual counsels. Appointments may be arranged directly with the lawyer concerned.

About Us

Tungkol sa Amin

209 Law Offices is not a partnership or law firm in the corporate sense, but a chambers-style setup — a group of independent attorneys practicing law separately while maintaining a shared professional address.

Each lawyer is solely responsible for their own clients and matters. There are no shared liabilities, and no lawyer within this office may represent or bind another.

What unites us is not a firm name, but a shared commitment to:
+ Uphold the dignity of the legal profession,
+ Observe the highest standards of ethical and professional conduct, and
+ Serve the administration of justice with quiet diligence.

“Each counsel in this office exercises the legal profession in a manner consistent with its highest traditions—measured, principled, and mindful of its public trust.”

Ang 209 Law Offices ay hindi isang legal partnership o korporasyong law firm, kundi isang uri ng "chambers" na kinapapalooban ng malalayang abugado na may kanya-kanyang praktis ng batas, subalit may iisang tanggapan.

Bawat abugado ay may sariling pananagutan sa mga kliyente at usaping kanilang hinahawakan. Walang sinuman sa amin ang maaaring kumatawan o pumirma para sa kapwa abugado.

Ang aming pagkakaisa ay nakabatay sa: + Paggalang sa dangal ng propesyon ng batas,
+ Pagsunod sa pinakamataas na pamantayan ng propesyonalismo at etika, at
+ Tahimik at tapat na paglilingkod sa katarungan.

“Ang bawat abugado sa aming tanggapan ay isinasabuhay ang propesyon sa paraang may prinsipyo, may pag-iingat, at may paninindigan sa tiwalang ibinibigay ng publiko.”
